1 ER Diagram (Relationships)
Category (1) ──< Product >── (1) Brand
      │
      └──< children (self-relation) 

Product (1) ──< ProductVariant >── Color
                     │
                     └── Size

Product (1) ──< ImageGallery

Product (1) ──< Review >── User

Product (1) ──< Slider

AcceptancePayment (independent)


Explanation:

Category: parent-child relationship possible।

Product: linked to Category & Brand.

ProductVariant: linked to Product, optional Color & Size. Unique constraint ensures no duplicate (product, color, size).

ImageGallery: multiple images per Product।

Review: multiple reviews per Product, linked to User।

Slider: linked to Product, with types like slider, feature, etc।

AcceptancePayment: independent, like payment methods.
